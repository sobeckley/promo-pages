Opari is a platform for collaborative ideating and creative problem solving which allows for asynchronous collaboration between users anywhere in the world. It is a place where creativity can thrive and where great inventions and solutions to problems of any size are born. Opari is not a replacement for traditional methods of collaborative group work and discussion. Instead, it is a tool that individuals and teams can use to employ technology to enhance the collaborative process and continue existing discussions when face-to-face collaboration cannot take place.

Freely share little bits of inspiration called Sparks

We believe that ideas don’t emerge as fully formed thoughts from one person on the first try, instead, breakthroughs start with a seedling of an idea, which evolves through iteration and collaboration.

In keeping with this philosophy, Opari is built around the ability for users to freely share and develop these seedlings, called Sparks, into larger ideas. Sparks can be presented in the form of problems, questions, or any bit of inspiration that users choose to share.

Users can share their opinions on Sparks through comments and categorize them with tags.

Combine and iterate on Sparks to make larger Ideas

An Idea is a combination of multiple Sparks, as well as their comments and tags.

With nearly infinite possibilities for Ideas made up of user-submitted Sparks, Opari is an environment where inspiration is never hard to find. The dynamic addition of new Sparks, user comments, or the identification of new problems or solutions allow Ideas to evolve through iteration.

Turn your ideas into projects, which can be managed right here or exported to elsewhere

Projects stem from one or more Ideas as well as user comments and tags.

As a Project develops, Opari tracks its progress and helps keep groups organized and on task with tools such as to-do lists. Projects are able to evolve and change through the addition of new Ideas, Sparks, comments and tags at any time, keeping the creative process alive.
